Alumni Online Community:

What is the Alumni Online Community?

BU's Alumni Online Community offers an active network that provides many ways for you to keep your social and professional connections with other alumni and Boston University strong. All degree recipients, and those that have been conferred with honorary membership status, are eligible for Alumni Online Community benefits including Alumni Events, Online Alumni Directory, and Career services.

If you would like to join the Alumni Online Community, but you do not remember your BU ID number, fill out this form. Our Alumni Online Community help desk will respond to your request during regular business hours at Boston University.

How much does the Alumni Online Community cost?


The Alumni Online Community is a collection of complimentary services available to all BU degree recipients.

I don't have my BU ID number. How do I login?
Your BU ID is the number by which you were identified when you were a student at Boston University. It is an 8-digit number with either U or a Z preceding. This number appears on your old student ID cards and on student loan statements. If you used your Social Security number as your BU ID when you were a student, BU has since assigned you a BU ID number to protect your privacy.

If you cannot remember your BU ID number, please fill out this form. Our Alumni Online Community help desk will respond to your request during regular business hours at Boston University.

I don't have my Password. How do I login?


If you cannot remember or do not know your Password, please visit www.bu.edu/alumni, select "Login" at the top right of the screen, and then select the "Forgot Password?" link. You will be prompted to choose a new password and answer the case-sensitive security questions that you setup when you initially registered. If you are unable to answer your security questions, you may reset your password by re-registering for the Alumni Online Community using your existing login name. To do this, visit www.bu.edu/alumni and select Register Now in the top right corner.

How may I change my Alumni Online Community username?


Once you have logged in to the Alumni Online Community, scroll to the bottom of My Page and select "Change my Login Name" that may be found under the Account Management Tools. You may setup a unique alias for your login name and Email Forwarding for Life email address. Please note that you will always be able to use your previous and new login names going forward.

Online Directory and other Alumni Benefits:

Where can I find the Alumni Online Directory?


The Alumni Online Directory can be accessed after you have logged into the Alumni Online Community. From the right hand navigation list, scroll over the second link that reads Alumni Community, then select Search Alumni Directory from the drop down menu that appears. This area of the website contains private information and is only accessible after password-protected login.

How do I change my address for all BU mailings?


The mailing address you list in the Alumni Online Directory is the address that BU will use for the Bostonia magazine and other BU correspondence. To update your address, login to the Alumni Online Community to your home page titled My Page . In the middle of the webpage, select the red "Edit" text next to Primary Address . Type in your any new information and then select "Update" at the bottom of the page.

Who can see my personal information?


The privacy of your personal information is our top priority. Only you can access and update your personal profile, and only Boston University alumni may view the Alumni Online Directory information. Through your personal alum profile, you may limit what information is visible to other alumni in the Alumni Online Directory. Areas of the website containing private information are only accessible after password-protected login.

How to I select what personal information is available in the Alumni Online Directory?


The privacy of your personal information is our top priority. You may limit what information is visible to other alumni in the Alumni Online Directory after you login at www.bu.edu/alumni. In the right hand navigation menu, roll over the link Alumni Community and select My Profile . In the middle of the webpage, select "Hide/Unhide Information" to view a checklist of the profile fields. Add a checkmark next to those items that you wish to suppress from displaying in your profile. You may update your selections at any time. To complete your updates, select Update Preferences at the bottom of the webpage.

Mailing Lists and Email Forwarding for Life:

How do I select what topics and events I want BU to email me about?


First, register for the Alumni Online Community at www.bu.edu/alumni. Once registered, login to your Alumni Online Community home page titled My Page . Under Account Information and Broadcast E-mail Settings , select the red "Change your preferences". Add a checkmark next to those categories of emails that you wish to receive from BU. You may update your selections at any time. To complete your updates, select Submit at the bottom of the webpage.

As a recent graduate, how do I keep my ACS student email account from expiring?


BU offers students accounts on the Academic Computing System (ACS) as long as they remain enrolled. Other Questions:

How do I post a job or internship for BU students?


BU Career Services will help you find great employees for your organization and help current BU students or recent alums at the same time. Jobs or internships can be posted throughout the year, at any time, at no charge. To post a job or internship online, visit Career Services online and click the Alumni tab on the upper right corner of the webpage.

Having trouble while using Internet Explorer 8?


Currently, the Alumni Online Community does not support Internet Explorer 8. If you encounter difficulty while using Internet Explorer 8, please browse using the Compatibility View by clicking the button located between the URL address bar and the Refresh button. The Alumni Online Community does support Mozilla Firefox, should you prefer to use an alternate Internet browser.
